Cumulative discount system

Ychangers offers you to make exchanges on our service even more profitably thanks to the cumulative discount system for regular customers. The discount amount is determined by the amount of successfully completed exchanges. The system takes into account all the transfers you have made.

Immediately after you register on our website, you will automatically receive a discount of 0.05%. To accrue it, you will only need to log in before starting the exchange. The discount is provided for the total amount of the exchange - if you exchange one hundred thousand rubles, the discount will be fifty rubles.

The discount is not accrued in cases where the exchange is negative for the exchange service.
